Gender Identity Markers

What do you mean by...?

Here at EveryKid, we know gender is a spectrum! We recognize that your anatomy doesn't determine your gender identity and neither does the gender binary. We have pulled some gender identity markers to help people find books that have characters that kids may identify with.

Cisgender

A person whose gender identity aligns with the sex that they were assigned at birth.


Gender Fluid

A person whose gender identity or gender expression is not fixed and changes over time across the spectrum.
